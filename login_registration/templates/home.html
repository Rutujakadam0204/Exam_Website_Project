We have created simple and user-friendly Pharmacology Practical exam online platform for Diploma in Pharmacy students as per ER 2020.



Key features of Exam Platform:
1. Very simple and user-friendly platform.
2. Similar to previous MSBTE Exam CD.
3. Included all questions related to new syllabus.
4. Questions may contain images/ videos related to questions.
5. Provided space for writing answers, which was not available previously.
6. You can take printout of student exams from “print result” tab.
7. To take exam in time bound manner, timer is provided for 30 min. for each exam.
8. Exam Platform included three sessional and one Annual exam i.e. overall four exams.
9. Exam platform is paid version and charged on the basis of charges per student per year.
